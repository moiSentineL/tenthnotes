# Definition
## Properties of Ethanoic Acid
- Commonly called Acetic Acid
- Used in vinegar (5-8%)
- Melting point is 290K
- It freezes, so called Glacial acetic acid.
- [[Functional Groups of Carbon Compounds|Carboxylic Acid]]
