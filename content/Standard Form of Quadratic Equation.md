# Definition
Standard form of a quadratic equation of variable $x$ is:
$$
ax^2+bx+c=0
$$
where $a\neq0$.
## Root of a QE
A real number $\alpha$ is said to be the root of quadratic equation $ax^2+bx+c=0$ if,
$$
a\alpha^2+b\alpha+c=0
$$
