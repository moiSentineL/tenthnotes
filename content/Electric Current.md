# Definition
Electric Current is the rate of flow of charges through a conductor. It is denoted by 'I'. 
## SI Unit
Its SI Unit is Ampere (A)
## Formula
$$
\text{I} = \frac{Q}{t}
$$
Where Q is [[Electric Charge|charge]] and t is time taken in **Seconds**
