Area of Sector?
?
$$
\frac{{\theta}}{360}\times\pi r^2
$$

Length of Arc?
?
$$
\frac{{\theta}}{360}\times 2\pi r
$$
<!--SR:!2024-05-05,94,280-->

Area of minor segment?
?
$$
\frac{{\theta}}{360}\times\pi r^{2} - \frac{1}{2}r^{2}sin\theta
$$
