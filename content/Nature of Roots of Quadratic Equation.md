# Definition
## Two Distinct Real roots
$b^2-4ac>0$
## Two Equal Real Roots
$b^2-4ac=0$
## No Real Roots
$b^2-4ac<0$

## Discriminant
$b^2-4ac$ is the discriminant of a quadratic equation
