# Definition
Mixing of a metal with another metal or non-metal to change it's chemical properties is called alloying.
## Examples
- Stainless Steel: Fe with Ni, Cr
- Bronze
- Brass
- Amalgam (which has mercury)
- Solder
- Steel - Iron and Carbon.
