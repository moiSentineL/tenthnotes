# Definition
Quadratic formula is used to find the roots of the quadratic equation $ax^2+bx+c=0$.
Formula:
$$
\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
$$
Provided $b^{2-4ac}\geq0$
## Roots found
The roots found through this formula are
$$
\frac{-b}{2a}+\frac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
$$
and
$$
\frac{-b}{2a}-\frac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
$$
