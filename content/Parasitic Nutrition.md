# Definition
Parasites are the organisms which absorb nutrition **directly** from other living organisms without killing them in soluble form. 
The organism which gives the nutrition is called a host.
## Examples:
- Cuscuta (amar bel)
- Mosquito
- Plasmodium
- Leeches
- Tapeworm
